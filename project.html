About Me

I studied fasihon in Busan and then Tokyo. Korea is full of tiny clothing shops in packed neighborhoods. Buyers flock to Seoul to buy fabrics and designs from the central Dongdaemoon market. It's an exciting and vibrant industry, where there is always room for experimenting with new designs.

I came to the US to study English in 2007. I met my husband here, and we both returned to Korea in 2010 for where we lived in Seoul and Busan over the next 4 years. My son was born in my hometown of Busan.

Now settled in the US, I love to shop online and take time to decorate a room right.
